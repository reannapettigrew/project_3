What is MyStory?












Story time has never been easier.


Create a story from our world-leading technology and feature your child's imaginative characters in their own story. The possibilities are endless.

You can choose to have your story narrated to you in a range of voice options, or you can read aloud. With our unique story prompts, each story is your own original creation!
